Delete Your Rallyo Account

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Rallyo account and associated personal data.

Application: Rallyo
Package name: com.mkayaaslan.rallyo
Developer: Mehmet Kayaaslan

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email from the address associated with your Rallyo account to mehmetkayaaslan@outlook.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Rallyo Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the username and email address associated with your Rallyo account.
  4. We may request limited additional information to verify that you are the account owner before completing the deletion.

Data Scheduled for Deletion

After the request is verified, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize account-related data under our control, which may include:

  • the Rallyo authentication account;
  • profile information, such as username, email address, biography, and profile image;
  • device notification tokens linked to the account;
  • group membership records;
  • live-location records associated with the user;
  • stored location-history records associated with the user;
  • friendship and friend-request records associated with the account; and
  • other account-linked application data where deletion is technically and legally available.

Data That May Be Retained or Anonymized

Certain information may be retained for a limited period where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, backup recovery, or protection of other users.

Content created in shared spaces, such as group messages, targets, or action pings, may be deleted, anonymized, or retained in a limited form where necessary to preserve the integrity of shared group activity or comply with legal obligations.

Processing Time

We aim to review verified deletion requests within a reasonable period. Completion time may vary depending on the amount of linked data, technical limitations, backup schedules, and legal requirements.

Before You Submit a Request

Account deletion may be permanent. After deletion, you may lose access to groups, messages, saved settings, profile content, and other account-related features.

If you only want to stop location sharing, you may revoke location permission in your device settings, leave the relevant group map session, or disable optional location history without requesting deletion of the entire account.

Contact

Email: mehmetkayaaslan@outlook.com

You can also review the Rallyo Privacy Policy.
