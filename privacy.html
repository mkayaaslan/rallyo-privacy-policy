Rallyo Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026
Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rallyo (“Rallyo,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Rallyo mobile application.

Important location notice: Rallyo is a group-based live location-sharing application. When you participate in an active group map session, your precise location may be transmitted to Rallyo's backend services and displayed to authorized members of your group. Location processing may continue when the app is minimized or running in the background.

1. Information We Collect

Account and profile information

When you create or use a Rallyo account, we may process information such as your user identifier, email address, username, profile name, biography, profile image, authentication status, group memberships, roles, and account settings.

Precise location information

Rallyo processes precise geographic location information, including latitude, longitude, direction of travel, update time, sharing status, and whether the application is active, in the background, or offline.

User-generated content

We process content that you create or submit through Rallyo, including group messages, map targets, action pings, responses, group details, profile images, and group images.

Device and notification information

Rallyo may process device notification tokens and related technical identifiers to deliver push notifications and maintain application functionality.

Local application data

Rallyo may store application preferences, session-related settings, selected map settings, and cached images locally on your device.

2. Live Location Sharing

Rallyo's primary function is to allow users in the same group to view one another's live locations on a map.

When live location sharing is active, Rallyo may send your precise location to Firebase Realtime Database. Your live location may then be displayed to authorized members of the group in which you are participating.

Live location records may include:

  • your Rallyo user identifier;
  • latitude and longitude;
  • direction or heading;
  • the time of the latest update;
  • your location-sharing status; and
  • the current application state.

Live location information is used only to provide Rallyo's group map, navigation, coordination, safety, target, and location-sharing functions. Rallyo does not currently use precise location information for advertising.

3. Background Location

If you start or participate in an active group map session, Rallyo may continue processing your location after the application is minimized or is no longer visible on the screen.

On Android devices, Rallyo uses a foreground location service for this purpose. An ongoing system notification is displayed while that service is active.

Rallyo may reduce the frequency at which location updates are sent or stored while the application is in the background. However, location processing may continue so that members of your active group can still view your current position.

Location sharing is expected to stop when the active tracking session ends, the relevant map session is closed, the required permissions are withdrawn, or the operating system terminates the tracking process. Device and operating-system behavior may affect exactly when a background process stops.

4. Optional Location History

Location history is disabled by default. A user may voluntarily enable it from the relevant Group Info settings.

When location history is enabled, Rallyo stores selected precise location points so that the user can review recent movement history on the map.

Stored location-history records may contain latitude, longitude, and timestamps. Rallyo is designed to retain these records for up to seven days. A scheduled backend process is used to remove records older than the retention period.

Turning off location history prevents new historical points from being recorded. It may not immediately remove location-history records that were already stored.

Rallyo does not currently provide a separate in-app control for manually deleting individual location-history records before the automatic retention period ends.

5. Groups and Social Features

Rallyo processes information about groups, group administrators, supervisors, members, friendships, invitations, and participation status to operate its group-based features.

Information displayed inside a group may include usernames, profile images, online status, live location, targets, action pings, and messages.

You should join groups only with people you trust. Group members may be able to view information that you share within that group.

6. Chats, Targets, and Action Pings

Rallyo allows users to communicate while using the group map. Messages and related metadata may be stored in Firebase Realtime Database.

Users may also create targets or action pings on the map. These records may include the creator's user identifier, geographic coordinates, assigned users, responders, completion status, timestamps, and related instructions.

This information is processed to coordinate group activity, provide navigation, and show group members relevant map events.

7. Third-Party Services

Rallyo uses service providers that process information on our behalf or provide essential application functionality.

Google Firebase

Rallyo may use Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Realtime Database, Cloud Storage for Firebase, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and Cloud Functions for Firebase.

These services may process account information, user identifiers, precise location, messages, group data, notification tokens, uploaded images, and related technical data.

Google Maps Platform

Rallyo uses Google Maps services to display maps, locations, targets, routes, and group activity. Google may process technical or map-related information in accordance with its own privacy terms.

Device operating-system services

Rallyo relies on Android and iOS location, notification, networking, and background-processing services. Your device settings and permissions determine whether these services are available.

8. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create and manage Rallyo accounts;
  • provide live and background group location sharing;
  • display optional location history;
  • operate group chat, target, ping, and navigation features;
  • deliver service and activity notifications;
  • maintain account, group, and membership functionality;
  • protect the reliability and security of the service;
  • detect and resolve technical issues; and
  • comply with legal obligations.

9. How Information Is Shared

With group members

Live location, profile details, messages, targets, pings, and other group-related content may be displayed to members of the relevant Rallyo group.

With service providers

Information may be processed by Google Firebase, Google Maps Platform, hosting providers, and device-platform services to operate Rallyo.

For legal and safety reasons

We may disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, enforce legal rights, respond to lawful requests, or protect users and the public from fraud, abuse, or safety threats.

We do not currently sell personal information.

10. Data Retention

Different types of information are retained for different periods depending on the purpose for which they are processed.

  • Location-history records are designed to be retained for up to seven days.
  • Live location records may remain stored as the latest known location until they are overwritten or removed.
  • Account information, group records, messages, targets, pings, and uploaded images may be retained while the account or related service data remains active.
  • Notification tokens may be retained until replaced, invalidated, or removed.
  • Local preferences and cached files may remain on the device until the application data is cleared or the application is uninstalled.

Rallyo is being updated to provide additional account and data-deletion controls. Until those controls are available, users may contact us to ask questions about deletion of their information.

11. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards intended to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Information transmitted between Rallyo and Firebase services is generally protected using encrypted network connections.

No application, network, database, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should protect their account credentials and should not share sensitive information with groups they do not trust.

12. Your Choices and Controls

You may:

  • grant or deny device location permissions;
  • disable location permissions from device settings;
  • enable or disable optional location history;
  • leave groups or stop participating in map sessions;
  • change permitted profile information;
  • control notification permissions through device settings; and
  • contact us regarding access, correction, or deletion requests.

Disabling a permission may prevent related Rallyo features from functioning correctly.

Rallyo does not currently include a complete in-app account deletion process. This functionality is planned for a future release. Account or data-related questions may be sent to the contact address below.

13. Children's Privacy

Rallyo is not directed to children under the minimum age required to consent to data processing in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

A parent or guardian who believes that a child has provided personal information without appropriate authorization may contact us.

14. Future Advertising and In-App Purchases

Rallyo is currently provided without advertising and without paid subscriptions.

Future versions may introduce advertising, subscriptions, or in-app purchases, including an option to remove advertising. Before such features are introduced, this Privacy Policy and the application's Google Play Data Safety disclosures will be updated to describe any additional information processing.

Precise location will not be used solely for advertising or analytics.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when Rallyo's features, service providers, legal obligations, or information-handling practices change.

The updated version will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Material changes may also be communicated within the application where appropriate.
