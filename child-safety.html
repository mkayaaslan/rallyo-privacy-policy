Rallyo Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: July 2026

Rallyo is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, or any content or behavior that exploits or endangers children.

Our Commitment

Rallyo strictly prohibits any content, communication, or activity involving:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Child sexual exploitation
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Any form of child abuse or exploitation
  • Content encouraging illegal activity involving minors

User Reporting

If users encounter content or behavior that may violate these standards, they should immediately contact us using the email address below. Reports are reviewed as quickly as possible.

Enforcement

When we become aware of content or behavior that violates these standards, we may:

  • Remove offending content.
  • Suspend or permanently terminate user accounts.
  • Restrict access to Rallyo services.
  • Preserve information where legally required.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement authorities when required by law.

Compliance

Rallyo complies with applicable child safety laws and Google's Child Safety Standards Policy. We continuously review our policies and practices to help protect our users.
